Traveling to France

France is the top tourist destination in the planet. On average, there are over 90 million visitors each year. It attracts visitors, with some of the most well-known tourist attractions in the world, top-notch art, architecture, mouthwatering food, spectacular beaches, wealthy ski resorts, stunning scenery, and an unbelievable amount of history.
